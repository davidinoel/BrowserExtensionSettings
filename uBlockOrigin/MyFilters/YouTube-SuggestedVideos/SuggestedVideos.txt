!===================================================================================================
! Here are the uBlock Origin filters I've been using to block the Recommended Videos
! YouTube rolled out in 2016 that they didn't build an on/off switch for, which seems odd.
!
! These rules block the video previews that appear within videos and act as a sort of
! "second generation" of the used and abused "Annotations".
!
! YouTube uses 4 keywords in a number of different ways that all have to be blocked.
! The keywords are: "channel", "video", "playlist", and "website", which YouTube places
! throughout the DOM in various locations, for whatever reason.
!
! There are also a few extra elements that have to be blocked that I add rules for 
! down below.
!
! YouTube only seems to support video player sizes of 640, 853, and 1280. 
! There may be other sizes (I've tried it on various resolutions), but this is all 
! I've encountered. If these don't work for you, please modify, contribute additions, 
! push the changes, and I'll look them over and merge them ASAP.
!
!
! uBlockOrigin uses CSS selectors, and from what I understand CSS doesn't support 
! wildcards, so while I am a programmer, this seems incredibly inefficient.
! 
! I'm a CSS novice, so if any of you are pro's, please feel free to optimize these
! (if possible) and contribute the changes.
!
! Many thanks,
! David Noel
!
!===================================================================================================
!
! As a footnote, the built-in YouTube filters to block comments and 
! the videos on the sidebar are incorrect.
! 
! To block the comments section use the filter:
! www.youtube.com###watch-discussion
! 
! To block the side-bar use the filter:
! www.youtube.com###watch7-sidebar-contents
! 
! ...and a great one everyone should be using to block all Annotations across YouTube use:
! youtube.com/annotations_
!
